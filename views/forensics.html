What is Forensics?

Forensics in cybersecurity involves investigating digital devices, files, or systems to uncover evidence of malicious activity or hidden data. It's like being a detective for computers, figuring out what happened, how it happened, and who did it. Newbies can think of it as "digging into files, logs, or metadata to solve a mystery."
