What is OSINT?

OSINT, or Open Source Intelligence, is the process of gathering and analyzing publicly available information to gain useful insights. This information can come from websites, social media, public records, or any other data that's open to everyone. Newbies can think of it as "being a detective on the internet, searching for clues that anyone can find."
